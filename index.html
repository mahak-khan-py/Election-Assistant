Interesting Election Facts

Fascinating details about the world's largest democracy.

👥

Largest Electorate

India has over 960 million registered voters, more than the population of Europe and Australia combined.

🏔️

Highest Polling Station

Tashigang in Himachal Pradesh is the world's highest polling station at an altitude of 15,256 feet.

👤

One Voter Booth

In Banej, Gujarat, a polling booth is set up for just ONE voter who lives in the Gir Forest.

🖐️

Indelible Ink

The ink is produced exclusively by Mysore Paints and Varnish Limited and has been used since 1962.
